    Mailjet’s White Label email marketing solution allows you to easily offer a custom-branded email builder, contact database, template management, and sending infrastructure right in your product and service. Unlock new revenue streams, increase customer loyalty and product stickiness, and gain more brand control over email campaigns.

    Fully Customizable White Label Email Solution

    Our “building blocks” approach allows you to pick and choose what features you want to offer your customers, including Advanced Statistics, Segmentation, Multiple Languages, Templates, and Contact Management. With Mailjet’s White Label marketing software, you can build the ideal email solution for you and your customers.

    All Features

    Robust Account Management

    Using our White Label email marketing solution, manage both master and sub-account stats, templates, and contacts. With our powerful account management functionality, you can easily share templates across thousands of sub-accounts in seconds, control and restrict access as needed, and monitor analytics to help make informed strategic decisions for you and your customers.
